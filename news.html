What is happening

News

Milestones from the lab, newest first.

  • Jun 2026

    Dr. Lee serves as a committee member and Day 1 session chair for the 1st SEG Early Career Community Virtual Workshop, "Projecting the Future of Subsurface Characterization with Seismology" (June 29 to July 2, 2026), where our group presents three papers with collaborators at Hanyang University.

  • Jun 2026

    Congratulations to Dr. Prasad Pothana on his new position as a Petrophysicist at the Bureau of Economic Geology, The University of Texas at Austin!

  • May 2026

    Seven abstracts from our group are accepted for presentation at IMAGE 2026 in Houston, Texas (August 2026). Congratulations to Toby, Chukwudi, Jerrika, and our collaborators!

  • May 2026

    Our 2024 Geophysical Prospecting paper on basin-scale S-wave sonic log prediction is recognized among the journal's top 10% most-viewed papers.

  • Feb 2026

    In the SEG EVOLVE Carbon Solutions program, the HyPerVille team (UTPB, Hanyang University, and Texas A&M University-Kingsville) successfully completed its Sleipner CCUS project.

  • Dec 2025

    Dr. Lee receives a UTPB Research, Scholarly & Creative Activity (RSCA) grant for "Supporting Net-Zero in the Permian Basin: Feasibility of Seismic Monitoring for CO2-EOR and Storage."

  • Oct 2025

    Lab members Chukwudi Onyeakusi and Toby Ude-Akpeh present their research at GSA Connects 2025 and the West Texas Geological Society Fall Symposium.

  • Sep 2025

    Our paper on depth-constrained recurrent neural networks for S-wave velocity and geomechanical property prediction is published in Geophysics.

  • Aug 2025

    Dr. Lee is awarded an HSI-STEM grant for 3D geological modeling of the Permian Basin and serves as a faculty mentor for the NSF-funded Summer Research Experience for Undergraduates at UTPB.

  • May 2025

    In the SEG EVOLVE Energy Exploration program, the HyPer Geothermal team (UTPB and Hanyang University) successfully completed its geothermal project.

  • Jan 2025

    Welcome to our first cohort of graduate students: Chukwudi Onyeakusi, Toby Ude-Akpeh, and Jerrika Stark! Dr. Prasad Pothana also joins the lab as a postdoctoral researcher in petrophysics.

  • Sep 2024

    Dr. Lee joins the DOE-funded Permian Regional Carbon Sequestration Hub (CarbonSAFE) project as Co-PI.

  • Aug 2024

    The Geophysics & Geomodeling Lab opens at UTPB. Dr. Lee joins the Department of Chemistry and Geosciences as a tenure-track Assistant Professor, supported by a $200,000 UT System STARs award for high-performance computing infrastructure.
