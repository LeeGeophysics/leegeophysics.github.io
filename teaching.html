What we teach

Teaching

Courses and mentoring at UTPB.

Dr. Lee teaches geophysics, geology, and data science courses at both the undergraduate and graduate levels at UTPB. The curriculum spans foundational geology to advanced quantitative methods, with an emphasis on hands-on data analysis, Python programming, and industry software in upper-level and graduate courses.

Undergraduate courses

  • Physical Geology
  • Geophysics
  • Exploration Geophysics
  • Structural Geology

Graduate courses

  • Data Analysis and Machine Learning
  • Mapping and Cartography
  • Quantitative Interpretation

Beyond the classroom

Mentoring and advising

Dr. Lee serves as the Geology Graduate Program Coordinator and on the Graduate Program Planning Committee at UTPB, and as faculty advisor for the UTPB SEG-AAPG Student Chapter and the SEG EVOLVE teams. Before joining UTPB, he served as a teaching assistant at The University of Texas at Dallas and Hanyang University for courses in reflection seismology, seismic inversion, applied geophysics, and digital signal processing.
